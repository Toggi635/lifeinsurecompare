Editorial Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

At LifeInsureCompare, we are committed to providing accurate, well-researched, and trustworthy life insurance information. This Editorial Policy outlines our standards for content creation, fact-checking, corrections, and updates.

Our Standards

  • Accuracy: All claims, statistics, and policy details are verified against primary sources before publication.
  • Independence: Our content is not influenced by advertisers, partners, or insurance companies. Advertisements are clearly labeled and separated from editorial content.
  • Transparency: We clearly distinguish between facts, opinions, and general information. Our writers, reviewers, and any conflicts of interest are disclosed.
  • Completeness: We strive to present balanced information, including both advantages and disadvantages of insurance products.

Research and Sources

Our content is researched using the following types of sources:

  • Official insurance company prospectuses and policy documents
  • NAIC (National Association of Insurance Commissioners) guidelines and reports
  • A.M. Best, Moody's, Standard & Poor's, and Fitch financial strength ratings
  • J.D. Power customer satisfaction studies
  • State insurance department regulatory filings
  • U.S. government data (CDC, Social Security Administration, Federal Reserve)

Fact-Checking Process

Every article published on LifeInsureCompare undergoes the following review:

  1. Research phase: All data points are traced to original sources.
  2. Writing phase: The author drafts the article with inline citations.
  3. Review phase: A licensed insurance professional reviews the content for accuracy and completeness.
  4. Final check: The managing editor reviews formatting, citations, and compliance with editorial standards.

Update Frequency

Life insurance rates, company ratings, and regulations change over time. We review and update our articles regularly to ensure they reflect current information. The "Last updated" date at the top of each page indicates the most recent review.

Content Reviewers

All LifeInsureCompare content is reviewed by licensed insurance professionals before publication. Our reviewers have experience in life insurance underwriting, policy analysis, and consumer education.

Reporting an Error

If you find an error in our content, please contact us at contact@lifeinsurecompare.com with the page URL, a description of the error, and a source for the correct information. We take all correction requests seriously.
