Frequently Asked Questions

What does AD&D insurance cover?

Pays a benefit if you die or lose limbs, sight, speech, or hearing due to an accident.

How is AD&D different from life insurance?

AD&D only covers accidental death, not illness or natural causes. Life insurance covers all causes of death.

Is AD&D cheaper than life insurance?

Yes, much cheaper, but coverage is limited to accidents which account for only about 6% of deaths.

Should I buy AD&D instead of life insurance?

No, AD&D should supplement, not replace, life insurance. Most deaths are from natural causes.

Do I need AD&D if I have life insurance?

Only if you want the extra accident-specific coverage. Standard life insurance is more comprehensive.
