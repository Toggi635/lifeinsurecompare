3. How Final Expense Insurance Works

Final expense insurance is permanent whole life coverage with fixed premiums. The death benefit is paid to your beneficiary for any purpose. Most policies use simplified issue underwriting with health questions but no medical exam.

Important: Name a specific beneficiary rather than your estate to avoid probate delays.
