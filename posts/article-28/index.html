1. What Is Key Person Life Insurance?

Key person life insurance is a policy a business purchases on the life of an essential employee. If the key person dies, the death benefit compensates the business for financial losses.

2. Why Every Business Needs Key Person Coverage

Key employees drive revenue and possess critical knowledge. Their unexpected death can devastate a business. Key person insurance covers recruitment, training, and lost revenue.

3. How Key Person Insurance Works

The business applies, pays premiums, and is the beneficiary. Coverage typically ranges from 5 to 10 times the key person's annual compensation.

Coverage Calculation: Methods include cost to replace, contribution to profits, and economic value approach.
