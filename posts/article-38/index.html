1. Life Insurance for Overweight Individuals

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a key factor in life insurance underwriting. In 2026, many insurers offer competitive rates for overweight individuals, especially those who are otherwise healthy.

2. Best Companies for Higher BMI

Transamerica and Prudential have higher BMI limits for standard rates. John Hancock's Vitality program rewards healthy behaviors regardless of BMI.

Best for High BMI: Transamerica. Best Wellness Program: John Hancock.

3. How BMI Affects Premiums

Most insurers use standard BMI charts. Normal BMI (18.5-24.9) gets best rates. Overweight (25-29.9) may get preferred rates. Obese (30+) increasingly impacts rates.

Note: BMI is just one factor. Overall health, including blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar, also significantly impacts rates.
