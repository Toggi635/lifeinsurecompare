Frequently Asked Questions

What happens without a buy-sell agreement?

The deceased owner's shares may pass to heirs who cannot or will not run the business, causing conflict.

Which structure is better - cross-purchase or entity?

Cross-purchase gives surviving owners a tax basis step-up. Entity purchase is simpler to administer.

How is the business valued?

Common methods include formula-based (multiples of earnings), appraised value, or agreed-upon fixed price.

Are buy-sell life insurance premiums deductible?

No, but the death benefit is received tax-free, providing immediate cash for the purchase.

How often should we review the agreement?

At least annually, or whenever significant business changes occur.
