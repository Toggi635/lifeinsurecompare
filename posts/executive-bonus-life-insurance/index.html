Frequently Asked Questions

How does an executive bonus plan work?

The employer pays premiums on a policy owned by the executive. Premiums are tax-deductible to the business and taxable income to the executive.

Why use a Section 162 plan?

It is simpler than non-qualified deferred compensation and provides tax-deductible contributions for the employer.

Who can participate in an executive bonus plan?

Employers can select any employee or group of employees, making it flexible for key talent retention.

Is the death benefit taxable to the executive's family?

No, the death benefit is received income tax-free by the executive's beneficiaries.

How does this compare to a split-dollar plan?

Executive bonus is simpler and more transparent, but the executive pays tax on premiums.
