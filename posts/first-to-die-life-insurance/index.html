Frequently Asked Questions

When does first-to-die pay out?

The death benefit is paid when the first insured person dies. Coverage then ends for both.

What is first-to-die used for?

Often used by married couples or business partners where the first death creates financial need.

Is first-to-die cheaper than two individual policies?

Usually yes, because the insurer only expects to pay one claim.

Can surviving partners get new coverage?

After the first death, the surviving insured needs to qualify for new coverage if needed.

Is first-to-die still commonly sold?

Less common than individual policies, but still available from several major insurers.
