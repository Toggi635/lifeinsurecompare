Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average cap rate on an IUL policy?

The average cap rate typically ranges from 10% to 14% for the S&P 500 index account, though rates vary by insurer.

Can I lose money in an IUL policy?

IUL policies guarantee a minimum floor of 0% to 2%, meaning you will not lose cash value due to negative index performance.

How is IUL different from VUL?

IUL credits interest based on an index with a guaranteed floor, while VUL directly invests in sub-accounts with full market risk.

What happens to IUL cash value at death?

The insurance company typically retains the cash value and pays only the death benefit.

Is IUL a good retirement vehicle?

It can be an effective retirement supplement for those who have maxed out traditional retirement accounts.
