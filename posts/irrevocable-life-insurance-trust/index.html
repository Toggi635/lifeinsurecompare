Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main purpose of an ILIT?

To remove life insurance proceeds from your taxable estate, avoiding estate taxes.

Who should consider an ILIT?

Anyone with an estate approaching or exceeding federal or state estate tax exemptions.

Can I change an ILIT once established?

ILITs are irrevocable, meaning they generally cannot be changed or revoked after creation.

Who owns the policy in an ILIT?

The trust owns the policy, not the insured individual. The trust is also the beneficiary.

Does an ILIT require professional setup?

Yes, always work with an estate planning attorney to properly establish an ILIT.
