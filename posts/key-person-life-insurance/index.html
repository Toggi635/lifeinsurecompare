Frequently Asked Questions

Who is considered a key person?

Any employee whose loss would cause significant financial harm - CEO, top salesperson, lead engineer, etc.

How much coverage do I need?

Typically 5-10x the key person's salary or a multiple of their revenue contribution.

Who owns and pays for the policy?

The business owns the policy, pays the premiums, and is the beneficiary.

Are premiums tax-deductible?

No, premiums are not deductible. However, death benefits are received tax-free.

What happens if the key person leaves?

The business can surrender the policy for cash value or sell it to the departing employee.
