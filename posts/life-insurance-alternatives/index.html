This section explores key aspects of why consider alternatives? and how it applies to your specific situation. Understanding these concepts will help you make informed decisions about your life insurance coverage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best alternative to life insurance?

It depends on your needs. AD&D covers accidents, critical illness covers specific diseases, disability covers income loss.

Is AD&D cheaper than life insurance?

Yes, AD&D is much cheaper but only covers accidental death, not natural causes.

Can critical illness insurance replace life insurance?

No, they serve different purposes. Critical illness pays you if you get sick, not your beneficiaries.

What is self-insurance?

Building sufficient savings and investments to cover your family's needs without insurance.

Should I buy alternatives instead of life insurance?

Alternatives supplement but rarely replace the need for term or permanent life insurance.
