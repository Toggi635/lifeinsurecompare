Frequently Asked Questions

Do college students need life insurance?

Generally only if they have dependents, cosigned loans, or want to lock in low rates for the future.

What type of policy is best for students?

A small term life policy (10-20 year) is affordable and provides a foundation for future coverage.

Can parents buy life insurance on a student?

Yes, with the student's consent and insurable interest from the parent.
