Frequently Asked Questions

Can I keep US life insurance if I move abroad?

Yes, most policies remain in force as long as premiums are paid.

Do expats pay more?

US-based policies at US rates. International policies may have higher premiums.

Is US life insurance valid if I die abroad?

Yes, subject to policy terms. Some countries may have exclusions.

Can I buy US insurance if I never lived in the US?

Difficult without a US address, SSN, and established ties.

Should I buy in my host country?

Consider both options. US policies offer familiar terms and tax treatment.
