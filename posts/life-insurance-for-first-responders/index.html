Frequently Asked Questions

Do first responders pay more?

Yes, due to occupational hazards, but rates vary by specific role and insurer.

Can police officers get standard rates?

Yes, some insurers offer standard rates for law enforcement with clean records.

Do firefighters face higher rates?

Firefighting is considered higher risk. Volunteer firefighters may get better rates.

What about EMS workers?

Paramedics and EMTs typically fare better than police or fire.

Is group coverage through the department enough?

Often limited to 1-2x salary. Individual policies supplement coverage.
