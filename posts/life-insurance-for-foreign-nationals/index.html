Frequently Asked Questions

Can a foreign national without an SSN get insurance?

Some insurers accept an ITIN in place of an SSN.

What visa types qualify?

H-1B, L-1, O-1, F-1 (OPT), and green card holders generally qualify. Tourist visas typically do not.

How much coverage can they buy?

Most insurers offer up to million for qualified foreign nationals.

Do they pay higher rates?

Rates are generally the same as for US citizens if all requirements are met.

What happens if they leave the US?

Most policies remain in force as long as premiums are paid.
