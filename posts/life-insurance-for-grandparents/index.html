Frequently Asked Questions

Can grandparents buy insurance on grandchildren?

Generally yes with insurable interest, but parent consent may be needed.

What if I name a minor as beneficiary?

The court appoints a guardian. Using a trust provides more control.

How much coverage do grandparents need?

- for final expenses, - per grandchild for education.

Can grandparents with health issues get coverage?

Yes, guaranteed issue accepts all applicants with a 2-year waiting period.

Is the death benefit tax-free?

Yes, life insurance death benefits are generally income tax-free.
