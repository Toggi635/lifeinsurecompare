Frequently Asked Questions

At what BMI do life insurance rates increase?

Most insurers start applying rate increases at BMI 30+. Rates increase significantly above BMI 35.

Can I get life insurance with BMI over 40?

Yes, but at substandard rates. Some insurers specialize in coverage for higher BMIs.

Does losing weight help my rates?

Yes, if you lose weight and maintain it, you can often qualify for reclassification to lower rates.

What if I have obesity-related health conditions?

Conditions like diabetes or sleep apnea will compound rate increases.

Will I need a medical exam?

Yes, height and weight measurements are part of the standard paramedical exam.
