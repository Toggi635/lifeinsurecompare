Frequently Asked Questions

Do pilots pay more for life insurance?

Commercial airline pilots often get standard rates. Private pilots generally pay higher rates.

Does FAA medical certification help?

Yes, a valid medical certificate demonstrates health standards and is viewed favorably.

Does insurance cover aviation accidents?

Standard policies may exclude aviation death. Specialized policies or riders are needed.

Best coverage for private pilots?

Specialized aviation policies without exclusions from insurers like Pilot Insurance Center.

Can student pilots get coverage?

Yes, but rates are higher. Consider adding a rider after gaining more experience.
