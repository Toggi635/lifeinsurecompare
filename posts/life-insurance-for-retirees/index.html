Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get life insurance at 70?

Yes, many insurers offer coverage to age 85 or 90. Options include GUL, final expense, and whole life.

Is life insurance worth it for retirees?

Yes if you have final expenses, want to leave a legacy, or have a dependent spouse.

Best policy for seniors over 65?

Guaranteed universal life (GUL) offers lifetime coverage at lower premiums than whole life.

Do I need a medical exam at 70?

Not necessarily. No-exam options include simplified issue and guaranteed issue.

Can life insurance pay for long-term care?

Some policies have long-term care riders that accelerate the death benefit.
