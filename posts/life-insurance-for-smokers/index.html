Frequently Asked Questions

How much more do smokers pay for life insurance?

Smokers typically pay 2-3x more than non-smokers for the same coverage.

Can I get life insurance if I vape?

Yes, but most insurers classify vapers as smokers, with similar rate increases.

How long after quitting can I get non-smoker rates?

Most insurers require 12 months smoke-free to qualify for non-smoker rates.

Should I lie about my smoking status?

No, lying is insurance fraud and will result in claim denial.

Can I get reclassified if I quit smoking?

Yes, many insurers allow reclassification after 12-24 months smoke-free.
