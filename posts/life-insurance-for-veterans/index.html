Frequently Asked Questions

What life insurance does the VA offer?

SGLI (active duty), VGLI (conversion after separation), and S-DVI (service-disabled veterans).

Can I keep my SGLI after separating?

SGLI ends 120 days after separation. Convert to VGLI within 1 year for guaranteed coverage.

Is VGLI more expensive than private insurance?

For younger veterans, private term insurance is often cheaper. VGLI premiums increase every 5 years.

What is S-DVI and how much coverage?

Can veterans with PTSD get private insurance?

Yes, many veterans with well-managed PTSD qualify for standard or near-standard rates.
