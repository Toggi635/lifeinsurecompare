Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get life insurance with high blood pressure?

Yes, millions of people with hypertension have life insurance. Controlled BP often qualifies for standard rates.

What blood pressure reading is ideal?

Readings below 140/90 are considered controlled. Below 130/80 is optimal for best rates.

Does medication type matter?

Some insurers prefer certain medications. Diuretics and ACE inhibitors are generally viewed favorably.

Can I improve my rates by lowering BP?

Yes, applying when your BP is well-controlled can result in significantly lower premiums.

Will I need a medical exam?

Most policies require a blood pressure reading as part of the paramedical exam.
