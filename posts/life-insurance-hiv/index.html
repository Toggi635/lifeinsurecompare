Frequently Asked Questions

Can people with HIV get life insurance?

Yes, many insurers now offer coverage for people with well-managed HIV, thanks to medical advances.

What are the requirements for approval?

Undetectable viral load, CD4 count above 200, consistent treatment, and no AIDS-defining illnesses.

How long do I need to be on treatment?

Most insurers require at least 1-2 years of consistent treatment with documented undetectable status.

Are rates higher for people with HIV?

Yes, rates are higher than standard but have become more competitive as more insurers enter this market.

What documentation is needed?

Recent blood work, viral load test results, CD4 counts, and an attending physician statement from your HIV specialist.
