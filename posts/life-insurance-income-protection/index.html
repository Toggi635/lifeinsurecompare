Frequently Asked Questions

How much life insurance do I need for income protection?

A common rule is 10-12x your annual income. This provides a decade of income replacement for your family.

What is the DIME method?

DIME stands for Debt, Income, Mortgage, Education - a framework for calculating total coverage needs.

Should I buy term or permanent for income protection?

Term life is ideal for income replacement during working years. Permanent for lifetime needs.

How long should income protection last?

Cover your highest-need years - typically until children are independent and the mortgage is paid.

How often should I review income protection?

Review after major life events: marriage, children, job changes, raises, or buying a home.
