Frequently Asked Questions

What does a life insurance medical exam include?

Blood pressure check, blood draw, urine sample, height/weight measurement, and health history interview.

How should I prepare?

Fast for 8-12 hours, avoid alcohol for 24 hours, get good sleep, and avoid caffeine on exam day.

What blood tests are performed?

Cholesterol, blood glucose, liver function, kidney function, HIV, and nicotine/cotinine levels.

Can I get life insurance without a medical exam?

Yes, accelerated underwriting, simplified issue, and guaranteed issue policies do not require exams.

How long does the exam take?

About 20-30 minutes. Results are typically available to the insurer within 1-2 weeks.
