Frequently Asked Questions

What is mortgage protection life insurance?

A policy designed to pay off your mortgage if you die, often structured as decreasing term insurance.

How is mortgage protection different from term life?

Mortgage protection pays the remaining mortgage balance directly. Term life pays a fixed benefit to any beneficiary.

How much mortgage protection do I need?

Enough to cover your outstanding mortgage balance plus 6-12 months of payments for transition.

Should I name the bank as beneficiary?

No, name your family as beneficiary. They can choose to pay off the mortgage or use funds elsewhere.

Is mortgage protection worth it?

If term life is more expensive due to health issues, mortgage protection can be a good alternative for home protection.
