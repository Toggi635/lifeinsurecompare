Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important rider?

The accelerated death benefit rider is often included at no extra cost and provides critical illness access.

Does a waiver of premium rider cost extra?

Yes, but it is relatively inexpensive and valuable if you become disabled.

What is an accidental death rider?

It pays an additional benefit if death occurs due to an accident (often double indemnity).

Can I add riders after the policy is issued?

Some riders can be added later, but others must be selected at policy issuance.

Are riders worth the extra cost?

Selected riders can provide significant value, but avoid over-insuring with unnecessary add-ons.
