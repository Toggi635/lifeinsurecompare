Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get life insurance after a stroke?

Yes, but most insurers require 6-12 months post-stroke with good recovery before considering coverage.

What type of stroke has the best prognosis for insurance?

TIA (mini-stroke) with full recovery has the best insurability, often qualifying for standard rates.

What factors improve my chances?

Full or near-full recovery, no residual disability, controlled blood pressure, and medication compliance.

How long should I wait to apply?

At least 6-12 months after the stroke. Applying too soon may result in postponement.

What documentation is needed?

Neurologist report, brain imaging results, current medications, and follow-up care records.
