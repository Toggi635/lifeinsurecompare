Frequently Asked Questions

What does long-term care insurance cover?

Covers costs of assisted living, nursing homes, home health aides, and other care services.

How is LTC insurance different from life insurance?

LTC pays for care services while you are alive; life insurance pays a death benefit after you die.

What is a hybrid life-LTC policy?

A life insurance policy with a long-term care rider that allows you to accelerate the death benefit for care costs.

Which is more expensive?

Standalone LTC insurance can be very expensive at older ages. Hybrid policies offer a way to fund LTC while preserving a death benefit.

Should I buy LTC insurance?

Consider if you have significant assets to protect and want to avoid depleting savings on care costs.
