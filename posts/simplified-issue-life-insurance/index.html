Frequently Asked Questions

Is simplified issue more expensive than fully underwritten?

Yes, typically 15-30% more because the insurer has less health information to assess risk.

How much coverage can I get?

Most simplified issue policies offer up to ,000, with some companies offering up to million.

What health questions are asked?

Typical questions cover tobacco use, recent hospitalizations, heart conditions, cancer, and other serious illnesses.

How fast is the application process?

Simplified issue can be approved in days compared to weeks for fully underwritten policies.

Who should buy simplified issue?

People who want to avoid a medical exam and are willing to pay slightly higher premiums for convenience.
