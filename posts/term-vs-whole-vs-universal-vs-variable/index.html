Frequently Asked Questions

Which type is cheapest?

Term life is the most affordable. Permanent policies cost 5-15x more.

Which builds cash value?

Whole, universal, and variable life build cash value. Term does not.

Which has the most flexibility?

Universal life offers the most flexible premiums and death benefit adjustments.

Which has the highest potential returns?

Variable life offers the highest potential returns but also the highest risk.

Can I combine multiple types?

Yes, many people layer term and permanent policies for comprehensive coverage.
