Frequently Asked Questions

Can I lose my cash value in VUL?

Yes, VUL cash value can decrease if your investments perform poorly, unlike IUL which has a guaranteed floor.

Which has lower fees?

Fee structures differ significantly. VUL has investment management fees; IUL has hedging costs embedded in crediting.

Can I switch between types?

You cannot directly convert. You would need to surrender and purchase a new policy.

Are they securities?

VUL is a security regulated by FINRA/SEC. IUL is generally not a security.

Which is better for retirement income?

IUL offers more predictable growth; VUL may offer higher returns with less predictability.
